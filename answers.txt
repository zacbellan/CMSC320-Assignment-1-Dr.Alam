Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how 
it might help

Answer: One field that data science could be applied to is healthcare.  
Doctors and nurses often have many patients and need to review large 
volumes of data.  Data science can help by analyzing these large datasets 
of patient records and other laboratory resources to identify patterns and 
potentially predict diseases early.

Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: The difference between merging and rebasing is that merging 
creates a new commit combining the history of each branch. Rebasing 
rewrites the commit by moving one branch on top of another.
